Where It Started

ONE STREET IN GULGASHT COLONY

OvenX opened its doors on 34-A Gulgasht Colony, Multan, with a simple idea: fast food doesn't have to mean cutting corners. Every pizza is hand-stretched, every burger is grilled to order, and every fry basket comes out loaded.

Word spread quickly — from students grabbing a quick zinger burger to families ordering the sharing platter on a Friday night.

Growing the Flame

NOW SERVING BAHAWALPUR TOO

The same fire that built our first kitchen now powers our Bahawalpur branch on Noor Mahal Road — same recipes, same generous portions, same commitment to made-to-order food.

Wherever the next OvenX opens, one thing won't change: everything is cooked fresh, the moment you order it.
